Renegade BBS is a DOS based Bulletin Board System.

Contact | Download | Documentation | BBS Links | BBS Documentary Entry 



Renegade BBS software development has changed hands several times.  
Quite often, Renegade BBS has been taken over by people who can't program and take the credit of others. 

It's best for you to pick the version that works for you.

Current sourcecode is on github. Rg has been ported to 32bit by Rick Parrish.
