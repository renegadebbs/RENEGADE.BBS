				<h3><a href="#">Welcome</a></h3> 
				<div class="subheader"></div>
<p> Renegade BBS is a FREE <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulletin_board_system">bulletin board software</a> derived from the<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Telegard"> Telegard BBS</a>  v2.5g source code (Which is in turn a modified version of the WWIV pascal  source code). </p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>  Renegade offers SysOps the  ultimate in BBS configuration, allowing your BBS to achieve a UNIQUE look and  feel.</p>
<p align="center">Renegade is a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_testing#Beta_testing">Beta test</a> BBS software, the current development team consists of T.J. McMillen and Lee Palmer		  </p>
